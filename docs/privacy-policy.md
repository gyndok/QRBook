# Privacy Policy

**QR Snap Vault**
Last updated: February 17, 2026

## Overview

QR Snap Vault is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

All QR codes, folders, and settings you create are stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account. Your data is never accessible to us or any third party.

## Data Collection

QR Snap Vault does **not** collect:
- Personal information
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Advertising identifiers
- Location data
- Browsing or search history

## Camera Access

QR Snap Vault requests camera access solely to scan QR codes. Camera data is processed on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Photo Library Access

QR Snap Vault requests photo library access solely to save QR code images you choose to export. No photos are read from or accessed in your library.

## iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your QR code data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is not accessible to us. Apple's iCloud privacy policy applies to this data.

## Third-Party Services

QR Snap Vault does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

QR Snap Vault does not knowingly collect any data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted here with an updated date.
