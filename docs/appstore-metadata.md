## Description

QR Snap Vault is the all-in-one QR code manager for iPhone and iPad. Create, scan, organize, and share QR codes — all synced across your devices with iCloud.

CREATE ANY QR CODE
Generate QR codes for URLs, plain text, Wi-Fi networks, contacts (vCard), calendar events (iCal), files, and payment links for Venmo, PayPal, Cash App, Zelle, and crypto wallets. Customize colors and add your own logo.

SCAN & DETECT
Use the built-in scanner to read any QR code. The app automatically detects the type and shows the right information.

STAY ORGANIZED
Search, filter by type or tag, sort by name, date, or usage. Create folders and add tags to keep everything tidy. Favorite your most-used codes for quick access.

FLYER GENERATOR
Turn any QR code into a printable flyer or poster. Choose from 5 professionally designed templates and export as an image.

BULK IMPORT
Create many QR codes at once using a simple JSON template. Perfect for businesses managing multiple codes.

SYNCED EVERYWHERE
Your QR codes sync automatically via iCloud. Access them from your Apple Watch with the companion app, or pin favorites to your Home Screen with widgets.

BUILT FOR iOS
Siri Shortcuts, Spotlight Search, Quick Actions, Share Extension, and Home Screen Widgets — QR Snap Vault integrates deeply with iOS.

PRIVACY FIRST
No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your data stays on your device and in your private iCloud. Period.
